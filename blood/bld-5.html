He grabs hold of your wrist the second it’s past the barrier of the doorframe. You don’t even realize he’s bitten into you until the weightless dizzying feeling of blood being drained out of you makes you lean heavily into the doorframe, your body sliding down it till you’re sitting on the floor. There isn’t a pause in Sam’s drinking as he crouches beside you, though you try to look away from the whole ordeal – the sight of blood always made you sick.
