And the feeding begins again in fervor. This time you do look at him, too shocked to turn away, and the thing you see at the end of your wrist isn’t Sam but a ravenous monster. That’s all you see though, before your vision finally gives out. You hit the floor with a thunk and through the haze of pain you feel the draining of your blood stop for a moment.
